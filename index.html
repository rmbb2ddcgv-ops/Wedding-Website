Our Story

A love written in the stars

Two people, two cities, one state that had other plans. We couldn't be happier that it did.

"Some paths lead exactly where they were always meant to."

Antonia left Boston for Denver chasing the life she'd always wanted — big mountains, wide open air, and runs that actually challenge her. Avery, one of her closest friends, found his way back to Colorado from San Francisco around the same time, drawn home by family and the resorts he grew up on.

They'd known each other for years, but something shifted the first time Avery watched Nia on the mountain — the way she skied, the effortless confidence of it, layered on top of everything he already knew and loved about her. That was the moment. One first date at Postino in Denver later, and somewhere on the walk home that evening, Avery told her he loved her. He meant it then and he means it more now.

They've been inseparable ever since — ski days, long hikes, and in 2024, an Aussie-doodle named Duffy who completed the picture entirely. On the afternoon of October 29th, on one of their favorite trails, Avery proposed. Nia said yes. And now, we celebrate.
